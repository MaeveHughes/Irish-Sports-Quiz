How to Play!

Challenge yourself to see how much you know about Irish Sports

The rules are easy:

  1. The quiz is multiple choice and there is one round of ten questions
  2. If you do not know the answer and do not wish to guess you may do so by just clicking the "Submit your Answer" button however you will not be awarded any points.
  3. You do not get deducted points if you get a wrong answer. So why not take a guess ;)
  4. You get one point if you get the correct answer
  5. You can complete the quiz as many times as you like
  6. Do your best but do not cheat!!
